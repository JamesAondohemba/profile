OVERVIEW OF BENUE TECH-SKILLS

The Benue Tech Skills Ecosystem is a groundbreaking initiative designed to empower the youth of Benue State with essential technological skills.
This program aims to create a vibrant tech community by providing comprehensive training in various aspects of technology, including programming,
web development, data science, and more. The goal is to equip participants with the skills needed to thrive in the digital economy and contribute to
the growth of the tech industry in Nigeria.

PROGRAM STRUCTURE

The training is organized into several modules, each focusing on different areas of technology. The lessons are designed to be simple, straightforward,
and highly interactive, ensuring that participants can grasp complex concepts with ease. The ecosystem also fosters collaboration and mentorship, providing
a supportive environment for learners to grow and succeed.
